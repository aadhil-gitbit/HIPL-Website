Hardex India Private Limited is a construction chemical manufacturer built on field experience and green chemistry principles. We make waterproofing systems, bonding agents, floor hardeners, curing compounds, admixtures, and industrial coatings — products designed for India's diverse climate and construction demands.

Our in-house facility gives us full control over quality and consistency. Every product ships with complete Technical Data Sheets and MSDS documentation. No resellers. No compromises.
