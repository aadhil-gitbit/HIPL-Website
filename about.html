Our Story

From a small manufacturing unit in Kerala to a pan-India construction chemicals company with over 150 formulations.

1980

Founded in Kochi

Hardex India established as a construction chemicals manufacturer, focusing on waterproofing solutions for the tropical climate of South India.

1990s

Product Line Expansion

Expanded into industrial flooring systems, protective coatings, and concrete repair products. Achieved ISO certification for quality management.

2000s

Pan-India Distribution

Built a nationwide dealer and distributor network covering all major metros and tier-2 cities across India.

2010s

R&D and Green Chemistry

Invested in research and development for eco-friendly formulations. Launched the Green Chemistry initiative with water-based and low-VOC products.

2020s

150+ Formulations

Crossed 150 product formulations. Expanded into specialty coatings, advanced primers, and decorative concrete sealers for the modern construction market.
